Movie 1


My personal Review :

"The Pursuit of Happyness" is a movie for anyone seeking inspiration, motivation, and a reminder of the power of human spirit and resilience in the face of adversity. It is a heart-warming, inspiring, and emotional movie that tells the real-life story of Chris Gardner, a struggling salesman who goes through numerous challenges and obstacles in pursuit of his dreams. The movie depicts the struggles of a single father trying to make ends meet and provide for his young son while pursuing his goal of becoming a successful stockbroker.

My favourite dialoge :

"You got a dream... You gotta protect it. People can't do somethin' themselves, they wanna tell you you can't do it. If you want somethin', go get it." When we have a dream to pursue something unique and share it with others, we may encounter situations where people do not appreciate or support our aspirations. It can be disheartening when we don't receive the desired response or enthusiasm from others. However, it is essential to understand that everyone has different perspectives and priorities, and not everyone will share our vision.

Learning to take away from the movie :

1. Hard work and determination: "The movie emphasizes the value of hard work, dedication, and effort in achieving success

2.The power of human spirit: "The movie demonstrates the strength of human spirit and the ability to overcome obstacles through resilience and determination."

Related concepts i've learned in the classroom :

Life is all about forgot the bad things happens in the past learn new thing in present and start preparing for future opportunities.
